Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

VoiceScroll is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

VoiceScroll does NOT collect any personal information, including:

  • Voice recordings
  • Personal identifiable information
  • Usage statistics
  • Device information
  • Location data

Voice Recognition

All voice processing happens locally on your device using Android's built-in speech recognition system:

  • Voice data is processed in real-time
  • No voice recordings are saved
  • No voice data is transmitted to external servers
  • Voice recognition works completely offline

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Required to listen to voice commands
  • Accessibility Service: Required to control scrolling and gestures
  • Internet: Optional, only used for downloading UI language translations

These permissions are used solely for the app's functionality and not for data collection.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • App settings (theme, language preference)
  • Custom voice commands
  • Downloaded language packs for UI translations

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

VoiceScroll uses the following services:

  • Android Speech Recognition: Built-in system service, processed locally
  • Google Translate API: Optional, only for downloading UI translations (not voice data)

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

Children's Privacy

VoiceScroll does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@voicescroll.app
